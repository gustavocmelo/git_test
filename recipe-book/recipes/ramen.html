Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pack ramen noodles
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup corn

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and add to the broth.
  5. In a separate pot, boil the egg for 7 minutes, then peel and slice in half.
  6. Serve the ramen in bowls, topped with the egg, green onion, spinach, and corn.

Enjoy your homemade ramen!
